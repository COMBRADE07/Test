Id Field Description Input Format
1 Loan A unique ID NA
2 Gender Gender of the applicant (Male/Female) Male/Female
3 Married Marital status of the applicant (Yes/No) Yes/No
4 Dependents Tells whether the applicant has any dependents or not 0/1/...
5 Education Tells us whether the applicant is graduated or not Graduate/NotGraduate
6 Self_Employed Defines whether the applicant is self-employed (Yes/No) Yes/No
7 ApplicantIncome Applicant's income e.g. 2000
8 CoapplicantIncome Co-applicant's income e.g. 1000
9 LoanAmount Loan amount (in thousands) e.g. 20 if you want 20k
10 Loan_Amount_Term Terms of the loan (in days) e.g. 180 for 6 months
11 Credit_History Credit history of individual's repayment of their debts e.g. 0/1 where 0 is low score and 1 is good one
12 Property_Area Area of the property (Rural/Urban/Semi-urban) e.g. Rural/Urban/Semi-urban
13 Loan_Status Status of the loan (Approved or Not) NA
